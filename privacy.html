Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how DailyQuotes (“we”, “us”, or “our”) handles information on our iOS application and this website.

What we collect

Analytics & advertising

DailyQuotes does not require user accounts. If we add analytics or advertising SDKs in future releases, we will update this page and the App Store privacy labels accordingly.

Third‑party links

The app or this site may link to third‑party websites or stores (e.g., the App Store). We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.

Children’s privacy

DailyQuotes is intended for a general audience and not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data retention

Locally stored data remains on your device until you delete the app or its data. Emails you send us are retained as long as necessary to address your request.

Your rights

You may delete the app to remove on‑device data at any time. To request deletion of messages you have sent us, contact rashad.shirizada@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated version on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact

If you have questions about privacy, contact us at rashad.shirizada@gmail.com.
